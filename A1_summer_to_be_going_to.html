📖 Grammar · Positive

I am going to… ✨

We use be going to when we have a plan or intention for the future!

✦ Positive sentences
Subject + am / is / are + going to + verb
  • 👧 I am going to swim in the sea.
  • 👦 He is going to eat an ice cream.
  • 👧👦 We are going to visit the beach.
  • 🐶 The dog is going to sleep in the sun.
💡 Remember the rule!
I → am  ·  he / she / it / name → is  ·  you / we / they → are
📖 Grammar · Negative & Questions

Not going to? Are you going to…? 🤔

Now let's look at how to say something is NOT going to happen, and how to ask questions!

🚫 Negative sentences
Subject + am / is / are + NOT + going to + verb
  • 👧 I am not going to watch TV today.
  • 👦 He is not going to play football.
  • 👧👦 They are not going to stay at home.
💬 Short forms: I am not = I'm not  ·  is not = isn't  ·  are not = aren't
❓ Questions
Am / Is / Are + subject + going to + verb + ?
  • ❓ Are you going to swim?
  • ❓ Is she going to travel?
  • ❓ Are they going to visit us?
✅ Short answers: Yes, I am. / No, he isn't. / Yes, they are.
