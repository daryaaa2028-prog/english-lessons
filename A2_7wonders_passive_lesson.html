💡 Quick Reminder (click to open)

Present simple passive: is / are + past participle  ·  "every year", general facts

Past simple passive: was / were + past participle  ·  "in 70 AD", "in the 17th century"

Present perfect passive: has / have been + past participle  ·  "since…", "for many years"
