📌 Your main techniques
Technique 1

Inflating Times, Costs & Amounts

Make a time, distance, or cost sound much worse or bigger than it really is. Often uses idiomatic phrases.

I spend half my life trying to fix this machine!
It cost her a small fortune, that bag.
I've told you a thousand times!
Technique 2

Repetition with and

Repeat a key word linked by and to suggest something goes on for much longer than expected.

He just kept going on and on and on about it.
I've waited years and years for this moment.
She talked and talked and talked.
Technique 3

Comparisons

Use like / as if / as though to create a vivid mental image through comparison.

It's like the North Pole in that back room today.
He looked as if / as though he hadn't slept for weeks.
She ate like she hadn't seen food in days.
Technique 4

Death Metaphors

Use words connected to death and suffering to describe uncomfortable or annoying experiences.

I'm dying for a coffee.
It's murder trying to find a place to park.
My feet are killing me.
